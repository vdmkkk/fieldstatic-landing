Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026 fieldstatic.shop

We keep this short and honest. Fieldstatic is a small operation — we collect only what we need to reach you when the next batch ships and to keep the site working. We don't sell your data. Ever.

01 Who we are

This policy covers the website at fieldstatic.shop ("Fieldstatic", "we", "us"), which currently offers product information and a waitlist sign-up. If you have any privacy question, email us at hello@fieldstatic.co and a human will read it.

02 What we collect

We only collect data in two situations: when you join the waitlist, and automatically as part of normal web traffic.

DataWhenWhy
Email addressWhen you submit the waitlist formTo send you one notification when the next batch is available.
IP addressAutomatically, with each requestSecurity, abuse prevention, and basic geographic context.
Browser / user-agentAutomatically, with each requestTo keep the site compatible and diagnose problems.
TimestampWhen you submit the waitlist formTo know when you joined the list.
Usage & analyticsOnly if you accept cookiesTo understand how the site is used and make it better. See section 04.

We do not ask for or store payment details on this site, and we don't collect special categories of personal data.

03 How we use it

  • To contact you — a single email when Fieldstatic ships again. No drip campaigns, no newsletter you didn't ask for.
  • To run and protect the site — serving pages, preventing spam and abuse, and fixing things that break.
  • To improve the product — understanding, in aggregate, which parts of the site are useful (only with your consent to analytics cookies).

Our legal basis is your consent (for the waitlist email and analytics cookies) and our legitimate interest in operating a secure website (for technical logs).

04 Cookies & similar technologies

A cookie is a small file stored in your browser. We use a minimal set, and analytics-style cookies only load after you accept them in the banner shown on your first visit.

TypePurposeSet when
EssentialRemember your cookie choice and keep the site functioning.Always
Analytics / observabilityMeasure how the site is used so we can improve it. Loaded by third-party observability tools.Only after you click “Accept”

Your choice sticks. When you accept or decline, we store that preference in your browser (not a tracking cookie — just a small flag) so we don't ask again.

Changed your mind? Clear this site's storage in your browser settings and the banner will reappear, letting you choose again.

05 Who we share it with

We don't sell your personal data and we don't share it for advertising. We rely on a small number of service providers to operate, and they only process data on our behalf:

  • Hosting — the server that runs this site stores your waitlist submission and serves pages.
  • Analytics / observability providers — only active once you accept cookies, and only to help us understand site usage.

We may also disclose data if required by law, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

06 How long we keep it

We keep your waitlist email until the launch you signed up for is over, or until you ask us to remove it — whichever comes first. Technical logs are kept only as long as needed for security and troubleshooting, then rotated out.

07 Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, to withdraw consent, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these — including getting off the waitlist — just email hello@fieldstatic.co and we'll handle it promptly.

08 Data security

The waitlist is stored in a dedicated database that's isolated from any other system, and the site is served over HTTPS. No method of storage is perfectly secure, but we keep the footprint small on purpose — less collected means less at risk.

09 Children

Fieldstatic is intended for adults. We don't knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has given us their data, contact us and we'll delete it.

10 Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle data, we'll update this page and revise the "last updated" date above. Material changes affecting cookies will reset the consent banner so you can choose again.

11 Contact

Questions, requests, or just want to say hi about privacy? Reach us at hello@fieldstatic.co.
